Multi-Airport Comparison Layout

Side-by-side panels with synced time axis. How should the grid adapt?

Responsive 2x2 Grid

2 airports = side-by-side (50/50). 3-4 airports = 2x2 grid. Each panel is a self-contained mini demand chart with its own TMI timeline, rate lines, and compact info bar. Shared filter sidebar applies to all.

Horizontal Strip Panels

Adaptive: 2=Side-by-Side, 3-4=Strips

Best of both: 2 airports renders side-by-side (each gets good height). 3+ airports switches to horizontal strips (each gets full width). Automatically adapts. Single airport = current full-size view.
