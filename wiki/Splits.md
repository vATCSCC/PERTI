# Splits

The Splits tool manages sector/position configurations for traffic management coordination.

**URL:** `/splits.php`
**Access:** Authenticated

---

## Features

- Area/sector grouping definitions
- Configuration presets
- Active split assignments
- Map visualization with sector boundaries

---

## Concepts

### Areas

Logical groupings of sectors (e.g., "West Area", "Arrival").

### Configurations

Saved combinations of area-to-position assignments.

### Active Splits

Currently active position assignments.

---

## Strata Filtering

Filter sectors by altitude stratum:
- Low
- High
- Superhigh
