# Acronyms & Terminology

This reference defines acronyms and terminology used throughout PERTI and VATSIM traffic management.

| Acronym | Definition |
|---------|------------|

| **DCC** | Command Center |

| **NOD** | NAS Operations Dashboard |
| **TSD** | Traffic Situation Display |
